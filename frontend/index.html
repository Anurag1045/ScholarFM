FAQ

Questions we actually get.

How is this different from NotebookLM?
+
NotebookLM generates a pre-recorded podcast. You can listen but you can't interact. ScholarFM lets you ask questions mid-episode — the hosts pause, answer your specific question, then resume exactly where they left off. No other tool does this.
What kind of papers can I upload?
+
Any research paper in any field — computer science, biology, economics, medicine, psychology, physics. Upload a PDF or paste the text. The AI works equally well across all academic disciplines.
How long does generation take?
+
About 15-25 seconds to generate the conversation script. Audio loads in the background while you start listening, so you never wait before it begins.
Is my paper data private?
+
Yes. Your uploaded papers are processed to generate the podcast and are never stored on our servers or used to train any model. Each session is ephemeral.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
+
Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel from your account settings anytime. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
